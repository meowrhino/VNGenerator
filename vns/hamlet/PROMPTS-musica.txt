MÚSICA Y SFX — Hamlet VN
=========================
Necesitas 8 temas instrumentales EN BUCLE (tono melancólico/gótico, NADA épico
hollywoodiense) + un par de efectos. Genéralos con IA (Suno/Udio) o pilla CC0/dominio
público. Guárdalos en  vns/hamlet/audio/  con el nombre exacto del slug (.mp3 u .ogg).
El motor los cablea SOLO si existen (drop-in) y no reinicia la música si no cambia de tema.

Consejo: ~1-2 min, que cierre bien para hacer LOOP, instrumental, sin voz, suave.

PROMPTS (Suno/Udio) — uno por archivo:

[principal.mp3]   (tema principal · 20 escenas)
Melancholic baroque chamber piece, solo harpsichord with soft sustained strings, slow and
sorrowful, introspective and tragic, sparse, instrumental, seamless loop, no drums.

[corte.mp3]   (la corte / palacio · 38 escenas)
Tense regal baroque, low strings and harpsichord, formal and stately but uneasy and
menacing, courtly dread, restrained, instrumental, seamless loop.

[tension.mp3]   (guardia nocturna / suspense · 8 escenas)
Cold dark ambient, sustained low strings and a distant howling wind, sparse and eerie,
creeping suspense, almost no melody, instrumental, seamless loop.

[espectro.mp3]   (el Espectro · 2 escenas)
Ghostly and ominous, deep cello drones with a faint distant choir, cold cavernous reverb,
supernatural dread, extremely slow, instrumental, seamless loop.

[ofelia.mp3]   (la locura de Ofelia · 3 escenas)
Fragile broken music-box / celesta melody, a sweet sad lullaby that turns subtly unsettling
and out of tune, delicate and heartbreaking, instrumental, seamless loop.

[lamento.mp3]   (cementerio / duelo y muerte · 3 escenas)
Funeral lament, mournful solo viola and cello, very slow, grieving, sparse and bare,
instrumental, seamless loop.

[duelo.mp3]   (el duelo final · 8 escenas)
Dramatic tragic baroque strings slowly building dread and tension, urgent but doomed,
dark and fatal, instrumental, seamless loop.

[marcha.mp3]   (el ejército de Fortimbrás · 3 escenas)
Distant solemn military march, muffled timpani and cold brass, grand and bleak, heard from
afar, instrumental, seamless loop.
